Munchkin Cat

Munchkin cats are known for their short legs, which are caused by a genetic mutation. They were discovered in America and in the UK. In 1983 in Rayville,Louisana a stray cat was found and resuced. It had kittens which half were born short-legged.

These cats are known to be playful and curious. They are a medium sized cat and live up to 12-15 years.
